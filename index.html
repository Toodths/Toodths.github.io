A lot of my love for the album comes from the lyrics however not only would it take me ages to go over the lyrics that i like it would also be a waste because literally anyone can search up genius.com and find much better analysis into the meaning of Rides vocals.

The guitar riff in the backing is also not only iconic and easily recognisable, its simplicity does not cripple its ability to instill a feeling of dread in the listener. This short exert is then immediately followed up by a soaring yells and a fast tapping drums along with slow heavy drum beat. This is the perfect set up for the vocalists, MC Ride, introduction.
